Vector Searching
====================

Like with the :ref:`vectorjoin` functions, the STL is starting to adopt some good 'search' functions for containers; but they are often cumbersome and difficult to use. The functions here are designed to provide a simpler interface to identify and locate the positions of elements within a container.
